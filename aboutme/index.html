About me: Isabel Lawton

Hi, my name is Isabel Margaret Lawton, but most people just call me Izzy. I am 19, go by she/her pronouns and I am currently studying ‘media journalism and publishing’ at Oxford Brookes.

My favourite animal is a frog and I have a hyper affectionate dog called Dewi, who’s back in Shropshire, where I am from.

I love art and I am passionate about mythology, creative writing and bringing people together through the use of activities and passions, and I know that thing like art or sport can do just that. For example, I used to be a teaching assistant for a children’s art class and netball team.

Publishing helps do that by making the works of others available to the public, which can inspire and motivate people to create their own works, creating a circle of creativity. This can be done throught the use of...

  • fictional/non fiction books
  • magazines
  • even posters for films or shows
